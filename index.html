üîç Research Overview

This research focuses on developing a Decision Support System (DSS) that integrates macroeconomic analysis (GDP growth rate, inflation rate, interest rates, money supply M2, unemployment rate, consumer confidence index, etc.), fundamental company analysis (financial statements, management quality, industry position, growth potential), trading timing (technical analysis and price action), market catalysts (earnings reports, policy changes), and market structure (market trends and liquidity) to provide comprehensive trading decision support for financial traders. The goal is to optimize the generation, execution, and risk management of trading strategies, improving prediction accuracy and market efficiency.

‚ùì Core Research Questions

1. Multi-Dimensional Data Fusion Architecture

How to construct an effective heterogeneous financial data fusion framework that achieves semantic alignment and temporal synchronization of macroeconomic, fundamental, technical indicator, catalyst event, and market microstructure data?

2. Predictive Model Optimization Theory

How can hybrid models based on deep learning and reinforcement learning effectively capture the nonlinear dynamic characteristics of financial markets and improve the statistical significance of trading signals across multiple time scales?

3. Human-Computer Interaction Cognitive Modeling

How to design human-computer interaction interfaces that can reduce decision bias and improve information processing efficiency through cognitive load theory and behavioral finance principles?

4. Data Quality Control Mechanisms

Facing the noise, missing data, delays, and manipulation issues in financial data, how to establish adaptive mechanisms for real-time data quality assessment and error correction?

5. Algorithmic Trading Ethics Framework

How to embed fairness, transparency, and interpretability constraints in DSS design to prevent algorithmic bias and systemic risk, ensuring market stability?
